Purpose

The purpose of this website is to inform the viewers about the weather conditions here in the Philippines. The weather can greatly impact our day to day lives by planning the activities or to do tasks daily through viewing it on your mobile devices or computers. Knowing the weather daily can help you prepare better for the day ahead.
