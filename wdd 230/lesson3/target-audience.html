Persona 1

Name: Brooklyn Page

Demographics:

  • Mother of 3
  • Married
  • Loves hiking and waterfalls

Scenarios:

  • She needs to check weather status for stargazing with the family.
  • She needs to know the weather on a specific month for a vacation.
  • Brooklyn walks to her job nearby and wanted to know if she needs an umbrella.
  • Brooklyn walks her dog every afternoon and needs to know the weather update.
  • She always set the timer on her sprinklers to turn on every 5pm in the afternoon, so she needed to check if it will rain today.

Persona 2

Name: Red Garner

Demographics:

  • On a vacation
  • Married
  • Loves ocean and island hopping

Scenarios:

  • Needs to check the weather to stay one night in a camp by the seashore.
  • He jogs in the morning with his wife and needs to know about the weather.
  • He wanted to know the weather to go for a scuba diving.
  • They travel at least four different places for island hopping and wants to know if the weather is perfect for island hopping.
  • The couple wanted to explore for caves and they need an information of the weather.
